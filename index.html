Angie's Blurbs

About me:

-Dolly Parton

I went to UC Berkeley, where I did activism around women's issues and double majored in Sociology and Gender & Women's Studies. I graduated college in 2011 when the recession was still strong. And there wasn't a lot of jobs in activism. I started working at an embroidery and screen printing company and was there 6 years!! I know too much about t-shirt printing, how to make jackets for fraternities and sororities, and how much companies make off selling you printed t-shirts at a concert. In 2016 I rediscovered my love for when I use to "hack" my myspace and write html tags, and decided to go to coding bootcamp. I moved to SF for 6 months and learned to code!!!!

After bootcamp, I worked as QA at a company called Internet Brands (they own webmd). There I transitioned to front end developer. After that company, I worked for a consultancy agency called 8th Light.

These days I like to keep up with new coding trends, watch tv (too much, I need to stop), listen to podcasts, and just this week I picked up roller skating. I also play my Switch but lately it's been collecting dust. I like to talk about wrestling, horror tropes and analyzing movies, current events around social issues. I really like pink and purple and watching videos about productivity tools I never end up using.
